--- STRENGTHS & WEAKNESSES ---
Strengths — Why We're Better
Our Unique Advantages
1. AI + Bookmaker fusion — No other bot combines LLM analysis with 30+ bookmaker odds. We triangulate: AI probability, market price, bookmaker consensus.

2. Multi-layer safety — 5 sanity gates catch AI errors before execution. Bookmaker blocking is unique to us.

3. Self-improvement engine — Autoresearch loop analyzes past performance and proposes config changes. No other bot learns from its mistakes automatically.

4. Candidate stock pipeline — Pre-analyzed candidates reused without AI cost. Saves budget, fills slots faster.

5. Calibration science — Brier scoring, prediction error tracking, confidence-stratified analysis. We measure what matters.

6. Dynamic everything — Kelly fraction, cache TTL, cycle interval, batch size, TP targets — all adapt to context.
Weaknesses — Where We're Behind
Gaps to Close
1. No order book depth — poly-maker checks liquidity, skips thin levels, steps ahead of best bid. We place blind orders. Critical for live mode.

2. No position merging — When holding YES+NO on same market, capital stays locked. poly-maker frees it automatically.

3. Polling, not WebSocket — We check every 3-30 min. poly-maker reacts in milliseconds to order book changes.

4. Still in dry run — All other legitimate bots are live. We haven't validated execution quality with real fills.

5. No on-chain monitoring — echandsome decodes Polygon transactions for whale tracking. We only see CLOB data.

6. AI cost dependency — $48/mo budget for Claude API. If budget runs out, bot is blind. Others have zero AI cost.
Bottom Line
Assessment
We have the best brain, they have better hands.

Our edge detection and risk management are superior to every repo reviewed. No one else combines AI + bookmakers + calibration + self-improvement.

But poly-maker's execution layer (order book depth, smart placement, WebSocket, position merging) is production-grade and we need to adopt those patterns before going live.

Priority: Validate dry-run P&L → then port poly-maker's execution patterns → go live.
